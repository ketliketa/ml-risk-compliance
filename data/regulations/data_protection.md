# Data Protection and Privacy Requirements

## Customer Data Security
All customer personal and financial data must be protected using industry-standard encryption and security measures.

## Data Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach, customers must be notified within 72 hours of discovery.

## Access Controls
Access to customer data must be restricted to authorized personnel only and all access must be logged and audited.

## Data Retention
Customer data must not be retained longer than necessary and must be securely deleted when no longer required.

## Privacy Policy Compliance
All institutions must maintain and comply with comprehensive privacy policies that clearly explain data collection and usage.
