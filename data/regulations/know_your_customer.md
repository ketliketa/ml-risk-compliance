# Know Your Customer (KYC) Requirements

## Customer Identification
All financial institutions must verify the identity of their customers before opening accounts or conducting transactions. This includes:
- Valid government-issued identification
- Proof of address
- Verification of beneficial ownership for corporate accounts

## Customer Due Diligence
Institutions must conduct ongoing due diligence to understand the nature of customer relationships and identify suspicious activities.

## Record Keeping
All customer identification records and transaction documents must be maintained for a minimum of 5 years.

## Risk Assessment
Customers must be categorized based on risk levels (low, medium, high) and appropriate monitoring must be applied.
